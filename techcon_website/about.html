History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for local developers. Over the years, it has grown into a major international event that attracts thousands of attendees each year.

Mission

Our mission is to create a space for knowledge-sharing, innovation, and collaboration in the tech industry. We aim to inspire both new and experienced tech professionals to shape the future of technology.
